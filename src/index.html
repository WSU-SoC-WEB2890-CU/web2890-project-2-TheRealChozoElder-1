A web resource for Beach family members and friends “Wisdom through knowledge, integrity and service”


Beach Haven is the name of a small town in eastern Pennsylvania. It’s on the bank of the Susquehanna River. The town was founded by Nathan Beach who purchased the property from descendents of William Penn in the late 1700s. Though few of us live in the actual Beach Haven, we can all strive to make our individual circumstances a haven of sorts. A good way to help in that process is to stay connected with each other. This idea was the inspiration of the Beach Haven Press (BHP) that began in 1994. It is also the inspiration for this web site.


From the Family Search Website


Beach

​1 English: topographic name for someone who lived by a stream, Middle English beche, Old English bece, a byform of bæce.
2 English: topographic name for someone who lived by a beech tree or beech wood, from Middle English beche 'beech tree' (Old English bēce).
3 Perhaps also an Americanized form of German Bisch .
